# Game Vision

## Elevator Pitch

The player starts with simple resource gathering, develops crafting skills,
builds a city as a production machine, and gradually specializes in specific
products.

Because nobody can be the best at everything, players need to trade with each
other, place orders, and build a reputation as producers of specific goods.

## Core Player Fantasy

You are not building an empire.

You are building your own craft city, which becomes known for specific products.

Example city specializations:

- furniture production,
- steel production,
- animal farming,
- food production,
- tool production,
- luxury item production.

## What The Player Competes For

The game is not about fighting over territory.

The player competes for:

- production efficiency,
- a smart specialization,
- access to customers,
- a strong reputation,
- a place in the server economy.

## What This Game Is Not

This game should not become another conveyor-belt tycoon where the main goal is
mechanically expanding the base.

The point is not to make the city bigger just because bigger looks better. The
city should be a functional tool that lets the player produce faster, cheaper,
or at higher quality.

## What This Game Is

This is a game about building a position in an economy created by other players.

The most interesting moment appears when other players begin to associate a
specific person or brand with a specific product.

Example:

> Mariusz Furniture
>
> The best furniture on the server.
